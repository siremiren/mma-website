Business Builder Workshop

A free half-day event for corporate professionals who are ready to stop thinking about starting — and actually start.

Four hours. A business plan, an offer, and a sales script — all yours.

Most people who want to build a business spend years thinking about it. Watching others. Waiting until they feel ready.

The Business Builder Workshop is built for one thing: getting you off the starting line. In one room, with a structured process, you will produce real outputs — not notes from a talk, not a summary of someone else's framework. Your business plan. Your offer. The beginning of your first sales script.

What you leave with

  • A completed, written business plan — answers to the seven questions every business needs to answer
  • A packaged offer using a proven framework, with a price attached
  • A customer avatar — the specific person you are selling to and why
  • Your first sales script with objections mapped and responses prepared
  • A primary marketing channel committed to, with a weekly action target

Who this is for

Corporate professionals — in marketing, finance, law, or any field — who have been carrying a business idea alongside a full-time job and haven't been able to move it forward. You do not need to have everything figured out. You need to show up and do the work on the day.
