Your Business Plan

Your 1-Hour Business Plan

Write quickly. Don't overthink. You can refine later — right now, the goal is clarity on paper.

1. What problem do you solve?
2. Who has this problem? (Be specific — name the person, not a broad category)
3. What is your solution — and how do you deliver it?
4. Why are you the right person to deliver this? (Experience, story, results, etc)
5. What will you charge — and why is that fair value?
6. How will you find your first 5 customers?
7. What does success look like in 90 days?
