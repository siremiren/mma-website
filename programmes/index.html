Programmes

From idea to first revenue.

Whether you want to start a business yourself, or you are an organisation helping others do it — we have a programme for you.

For Individuals

A step-by-step path to
starting your business.

Our programmes are for people who are done thinking about starting and ready to actually do it. Most of our participants are busy. They have jobs, families, and limited time. Our structure is built around that reality.

On-Demand · Self-Paced

Business Builder Accelerator

Learn everything you need to start a business — on your own time, at your own pace. Pre-recorded video lessons that walk you through every step, from testing your idea to getting your first paying client.

What you will learn

  • How to test a business idea and find people willing to pay
  • How to create an offer that solves a real problem
  • How to sell — even if you have never sold anything before
  • How to get leads when you have a small budget and little time
  • How to set up the basics so your business actually runs

Who this is for

  • People with a business idea who need a plan to make it real
  • People who are tired of reading about business and want to start doing it
  • Busy people who need something they can follow in short bursts

What you will walk away with

  • A tested business idea with a clear target market
  • An offer that is ready to sell
  • Sales scripts and outreach templates you can use straight away
  • A marketing plan that fits your life and your budget
  • A 90-day roadmap for your first three months
Done-With-You · Live Cohort

Business Builder Programme

Build your business with an experienced founder right beside you. This is hands-on. You will be coached, challenged, and held to account every week — from your first idea through to your first paying client.

What this includes

  • Weekly live sessions with direct coaching and feedback
  • Daily check-ins and progress tracking
  • One-to-one support on your specific challenges
  • Ready-made templates, scripts, and systems you use as you go
  • A week-by-week plan that builds toward your launch

Who this is for

  • People who want a coach, not just a course
  • People who are ready to launch within the programme
  • People who know they need someone holding them to account

What you will walk away with

  • A real, running business — not a plan sitting in a drawer
  • Paying clients you got during the programme
  • Sales and outreach systems that are tested and working
  • A 90-day growth plan for after you finish
  • Access to the Mind and Method community
For Organisations

Enterprise education designed for the
communities you serve.

We partner with universities, local authorities, government agencies, charities, prisons, and corporate organisations to deliver structured business education to people at the earliest stages of enterprise.

Universities

Enterprise education and employability workshops. We help students and graduates explore self-employment as a viable career path — particularly those from widening participation backgrounds.

Councils & Government

Self-employment support, economic development, and workforce programmes for communities where traditional employment pathways are limited.

Charities & Third Sector

Enterprise programmes for young people, refugees, people leaving care, or communities facing economic disadvantage. We design around your learners and report against your outcomes.

Prisons & Rehabilitation

Practical business skills and a structured pathway toward self-employment upon release. Designed for delivery within secure environments.

Schools & Youth

Age-appropriate business workshops for secondary school and sixth-form students, with a focus on young people from underrepresented backgrounds.

Corporate Partners

Career-transition support, intrapreneurship training, and community impact programmes for corporate organisations.
