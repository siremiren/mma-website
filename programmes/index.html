Programmes

Structured paths from idea to revenue.

Whether you are an individual ready to build, or an organisation looking to support your community into enterprise — we have a programme for you.

For Individuals

Build your business with structure,
clarity, and accountability.

Our programmes are built for people who are ready to move from thinking about starting a business to actually building one. Many of our participants have jobs, families, and limited time.

Step 1 · On-Demand

We Teach You How to Build It

A structured, on-demand programme delivering practical business education you can complete alongside existing commitments. Pre-recorded video curriculum covering every stage from validating your idea to acquiring your first client.

What you will learn

  • How to validate a business idea and identify a market willing to pay
  • How to design an offer that solves a real problem at a price people accept
  • How to sell with confidence — even if you have never sold before
  • How to build a lead generation system that works with limited time and budget
  • How to set up the operational foundations of a real, functioning business

Who this is for

  • People with a business idea who need structure to make it real
  • Anyone tired of consuming content that never leads to action
  • People with limited time who need a focused, practical path they can follow in short bursts

What you will leave with

  • A validated business concept with a defined target market
  • A complete offer ready to take to market
  • Sales scripts and outreach frameworks you can use immediately
  • A marketing plan tailored to your situation and budget
  • A clear operational roadmap for your first 90 days
Step 2 · Done-With-You

We Help You Build It and Launch It

A comprehensive implementation programme where you build your business alongside an experienced founder. This is hands-on, guided execution. You will be coached, challenged, and held accountable every step of the way — from first concept through to first paying client.

What this includes

  • Weekly live sessions with direct coaching and feedback
  • Daily accountability tracking and performance reviews
  • One-to-one support on your specific business challenges
  • Templated frameworks, scripts, and systems you implement as you go
  • A structured curriculum that builds week-on-week toward launch

Who this is for

  • People who want hands-on guidance, not just information
  • Anyone committed to launching within the programme window
  • People who know they need accountability to follow through

What you will leave with

  • A launched and operating business — not a plan, an actual business
  • Paying clients acquired during the programme
  • Tested sales processes and proven outreach systems
  • A 90-day growth plan for continued momentum after graduation
  • Ongoing access to the Mind and Method community
Step 3 · Done-For-You

We Build and Launch It for You

You have the idea. We do the rest. Our team builds your entire business from the ground up and hands it to you ready to operate. No guesswork. No learning curve. Just a business that works.

What we do for you

  • Register your business and set up the legal foundations
  • Build your brand, website, and online presence
  • Create your offer, pricing, and sales materials
  • Set up and run paid advertising to get your first clients
  • Connect you with accountants, legal support, and business finance

Who this is for

  • People who want a business but do not have the time to build it themselves
  • People who want to skip the learning curve and start with a running operation
  • People who are ready to invest in having it done properly from day one

What you will walk away with

  • A registered, legally compliant business
  • A complete brand and online presence
  • A tested offer with paying clients
  • Advertising systems running and generating leads
  • Full handover with everything you need to operate independently
For Organisations

Enterprise education designed for the
communities you serve.

We partner with universities, local authorities, government agencies, charities, prisons, and corporate organisations to deliver structured business education to people at the earliest stages of enterprise.

Universities

Enterprise education, employability programmes, and founder development workshops — particularly for widening participation backgrounds.

Councils & Government

Self-employment support, economic regeneration, and workforce development for communities where traditional employment pathways are limited.

Charities & Third Sector

Enterprise programmes for young people, refugees, people leaving care, or communities facing economic disadvantage.

Prisons & Rehabilitation

Building practical business skills, self-efficacy, and a structured pathway toward self-employment upon release.

Schools & Youth

Age-appropriate entrepreneurship workshops for secondary school and sixth-form students from underrepresented backgrounds.

Corporate Partners

Career-transition support, intrapreneurship training, and community impact programmes.
