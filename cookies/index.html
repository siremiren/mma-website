Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Mind and Method Academy Ltd uses cookies when you visit mindandmethodacademy.com.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They make websites function efficiently and provide information to site owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

Strictly Necessary Cookies

Essential for the Website to function. These do not require consent.

Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics cookies (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors interact with the Website. Placed only with your consent.

Functional Cookies

Allow the Website to remember your choices and provide enhanced features. Placed only with your consent.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Third parties that may set cookies include Google Analytics and FormSubmit.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect Website functionality.

5. Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time.
