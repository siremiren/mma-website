Meet the Founder
Photo: Professional headshot of Samuel

Samuel Iremiren

Founder & Lead Educator

Samuel has spent over a decade helping people start businesses across three continents. He holds an MBA and an MSc in Economics. He knows the theory — but more importantly, he knows what actually works in practice.

Before starting Mind and Method Academy, Samuel built and ran a property company that brought in over £15,000 a month. He has coached hundreds of people through the hardest part of starting a business — the beginning.

Samuel works with people who want to start but keep getting stuck. He understands them because he has spent years in the room with them. He knows what holds them back: the fear of losing money, the fear of looking foolish, and the gap between reading about business and actually doing it.

Why We Exist

Most business education
misses the people who need it most.

At one end, there are expensive MBA programmes and accelerators. They are built for people who already have money, contacts, and confidence. At the other end, there is endless free content — YouTube, podcasts, social media. It feels like progress, but nothing changes.

In the middle are millions of people who keep getting stuck. They have the ability. They have the ambition. They just lack a clear, affordable plan and someone to walk them through it. They try things in short bursts — a course, a book, an event — then go back to their normal routine.

We built Mind and Method Academy to close that gap. Real business education. Simple, practical, and affordable. No £30,000 MBA needed.

"They keep reading about business. They keep watching videos about business. But they never actually start one."
— The problem we exist to solve
How We Work

Everything we teach leads
to action.

We found seven things that stop people from getting their first paying client. Every programme we run is built to fix each one.

Practical, Not Theoretical

Every session ends with something you can use — a tested offer, a sales script, a marketing plan. You leave with tools, not just ideas.

Accountable, Not Passive

Daily check-ins, tracked progress, live sessions, and honest feedback. We make it hard to fall off track — even when life gets busy.

Affordable, Not Exclusive

Our programmes are priced for the people who need them most. We work with universities, councils, charities, and community groups to reach people who would never walk through the door of a business school.
